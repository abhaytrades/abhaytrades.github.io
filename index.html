// 04 — Strategy
NY DOMINATION STRATEGY
A fully rule-based, trend-following strategy built for the New York session. Every entry follows a defined set of conditions — no discretion, no guessing. The system has been validated through 100+ backtests and 100+ live executions.
01
Trend Identification
Higher timeframe trend direction is established first. Only trade in the direction of the dominant trend — no counter-trend entries.
02
Retracement Zone (Min 50%)
Wait for price to retrace minimum 50% of the last impulse move. This defines the entry zone — no chasing breakouts.
03
Execution Trigger
Entry via (A) market structure shift in trend direction, or (B) failed retracement confirmed by double top / double bottom pattern.
04
Confirmation (1m / 5m MSS)
Lower timeframe (1m or 5m) market structure shift confirms entry. Reduces false entries significantly.
05
Risk Management
Stop loss placed at lower timeframe high/low post-shift. Target at execution timeframe swing high/low. Fixed 1–2% risk per trade.
